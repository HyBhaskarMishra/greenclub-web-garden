Who We Are?

GreenClub is MNNIT's premier environmental sustainability club, dedicated to creating a greener campus and fostering environmental consciousness among students. We believe in action-oriented initiatives that make a real difference.

From organizing tree plantation drives to implementing waste management systems, we're committed to building a sustainable future for our campus and beyond.

Our journey towards sustainability through impactful initiatives and community engagement

2020 - Foundation

Established GreenClub with 20 passionate students committed to environmental change
